.discover(CS)

about() {

/* .discover(CS) was born out of a desire to grow the
computer science community. Computer science teachers
typically do not have specialization or real-world
experience. As such, we pair small groups of students
with mentors who have considerable skill in their
respective fields. We aspire to allow students to
discover the array of fields within the broader computer
science discipline. */

}
mission() {

/* The goal of .discover(CS) is to help young people to
discover the field of computer science. We hope to
connect youth up with mentors in specific fields. We
envision a future where young people are inspired by
industry leaders to develop and hone their skills in
computer science. We recognize that competency in
computer sciene will be a requirement for the careers
of tomorrow, and want to prepare our youth. This
is done through personalized mentoring in the exciting
and ever-expanding field of computer science. */

}
